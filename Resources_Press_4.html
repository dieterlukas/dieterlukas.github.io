What to do about the different reports?

Most reports were good. I think the mostly widely read stories are those by journalists that make an effort to ensure that their story is correct. Many other outlets publish the press release in full or quote from the release (or, in a form of reporting I had not encountered before, build a story by copying paragraphs from other news outlets).

I will only know about the bad reports after they have been published so there is not much I can do about them.

Though, all of these reports rely on information I originally release - the paper, the press release, email replies, or phone interviews.

So I should start early to prepare all of the material that I can control.
